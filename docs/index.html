Greetings, friends and fellow design nomads!

Thank you so much for visiting my blog and joining me on my newfound design journey at 8th Light. My name is Gina, I'm an east coast raised Filipina American and a midwest livin' designer with a passion for creative problem solving. As an avid hustler and independent learner, I know better than to ever start the day without a cup of coffee. I am a book lover, nature admirer, and an art appreciator. When I'm not busy designing the day away, you can either catch me on the weekends at a cafe learning something new, find me at a book shop with Neruda in a corner, spot me at an art gallery opening, or discover me trying out new restaurants.
